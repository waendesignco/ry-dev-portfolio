Accelerate

WordPress web development

Overview

Accelerate is a marketing agency that offers website design, development, and content strategy. Their goal is to support small businesses by making their clients visible and reaching the target audiences.

Objective

Accelerate requested to develop a website to showcase their services, projects, and blog posts. They needed a versatile platform to add feature projects over time and frequent blog posts easily shared by social media.

Approach

The design comp of the landing page was straightforward. I knew the additional contents would be handled by their team members, so I created page templates by advanced custom fields.

Ta-da!

The result is a clean landing page autoloaded with newer projects and blog posts. It produces a fresh glance every time you visit.
